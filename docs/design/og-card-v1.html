05

Rules

Don't deviate
  • Light palette only. Do not generate a dark variant — chat clients render OG cards on their own background.
  • One template, no exceptions. No alternate layouts for breaking news, no variant cards. The image is the only thing that varies per Story.
  • Title 2 lines max, deck 2 lines max. Truncate with ellipsis; never shrink type to fit.
  • Accent rule still holds. The eyebrow dot is the one accent use on the card. Never tint the title, deck, or "A" mark fill against the rules.
  • No URL on the card. Slack and iMessage already render the host (armal.news) outside the card. Putting it inside is redundant.
  • No date / no read-time. OG cards live forever in chat history; volatile metadata makes them age poorly.
